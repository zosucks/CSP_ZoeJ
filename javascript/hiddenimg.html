What is biology?

The word "biology", BIO - meaning life then logy - meaning the study of. In basic terms biology is the study of all life. With this broad terms comes many other smaller compartments that are more focused on one certain topic of life. Like as plants, animals, microbes, genetics, ecology, and evolution.


Some main concepts of this topic would be:

  • Organisms
  • Functions
  • Adaptation
  • Characteristics of Life
